Quick Daily Tips

Tiny changes that make a big difference when practiced consistently.

Hydrate Early

Drink a glass of water first thing in the morning to gently wake up your body and support digestion.

Half Your Plate

Aim for half your plate to be colorful veggies at lunch and dinner for fiber, vitamins, and volume.

Snack with Intention

Pair a source of protein with fiber (like yogurt and berries) to stay satisfied between meals.

Read the Ingredients

Scan the ingredient list: shorter, familiar ingredients usually mean less processing.

Slow Down

Take a few extra minutes to chew and savor your food—your fullness cues need time to catch up.

Plan One Meal

If full meal prep feels overwhelming, start by planning just one meal you’ll repeat during the week.
